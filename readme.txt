Provides the ability to display your PledgeMusic campaign on your WordPress site.

== Description ==

PledgeMusic is a platform that provides artists the resources they need to build successful long-term careers. 
Through our easy to use direct-to-fan model, we've fuzed advanced technologies and social media techniques with decades of music business expertise to create the most effective music fundraising, marketing, and sales tool in the world.

**Plugin Features:**

* Connect your PledgeMusic campaign to your WordPress site.
* Two display options: Sidebar widget, or lightbox popup.

**Sign up with [PledgeMusic](http://www.pledgemusic.com/sign_up/artist?referrer=wpplugin "PledgeMusic") now!**

**Plugin developed by [45PRESS](http://www.45press.com "45PRESS") - a digital marketing agency that specializes in website creation, development, hosting, & video production.**

== Installation ==

1. Unzip `pledgemusic.zip` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Enter your PledgeMusic ID.
4. Display your badge via sidebar widget or lightbox popup.
